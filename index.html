Who I Am

I'm Alex Marin, a 3rd-year undergraduate majoring in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at the Pacific Institute of Technology. My work lives at the intersection of deep learning research and scalable software systems.

My obsession is making neural networks that don't just work—they understand. I've trained models on edge devices, deployed real-time computer vision pipelines, and fine-tuned large language models for domain-specific tasks.

Outside the lab I'm a competitive hackathon participant, open-source contributor, and occasional underwater photographer — which is probably why everything I build looks like it came from the bottom of the ocean.

"I believe the most powerful AI systems are built by engineers who understand both the mathematics and the messy reality of production deployments."
